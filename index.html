Experienced & Reliable

Since 1971, R.L. Leek Industries, Inc. has carefully crafted its trusted reputation. What started as a simple transportation company from humble beginnings has grown into a full service, transportation, storage, and just-in-time logistics company.

Along with maintaining a long-term relationship with our customers, some of which go back well over 30 years, we also have a very stable driver base with an extremely low turnover rate. This ensures the development of good relationships with our customers and their long term goals.
