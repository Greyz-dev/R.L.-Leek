Our Services

Full Service Product Handling

R.L. Leek Offers full service product handling. Our warehouse staff is experienced in handling products ranging from standardized pallets to oversized, specialty materials. Our staff is also experienced in handling materials including packaging and corrugated materials, food products, drummed materials, and heavy equipment. Our staff is trained in and practices quality control and inspection techniques to ensure the quality of your product is preserved from the moment they are received until the time they are released for delivery to your customers. Inventory tracking and control services are available. Bill of ladings and inventory reports can be submitted to allow for "real time" inventory monitoring. We offer cross-docking and repack services as well as short and long term storage at competitive rates. Daily UPS service is also available.

R.L Leek located in the heart of the I-39 corridor with close proximity to Chicago and Wisconsin, approximately 30 minutes north of the HUB3 intermodal facility in Rochelle, Illinois. Our transportation division can accommodate any service needs including small package expediting, straight truck, dry van, refrigerated conestoga, flat, and step deck service going anywhere in the continental United States.

Every customer account will receive personalized attention regardless of the size or volume. We are owner-operated and are dedicated to providing efficient, quality services at a competitive rate. References are available upon request. For any additional information about any of our warehousing services, please visit our contact page at the top of the page.




WAREHOUSING

Leek Warehouse and Leek Family Properties are modern facilities offering a combined 250,000 Square Foot of food grade 3PL warehousing, including inventory control. Our facilities are clean, secure, well-maintained, and are officially licensed by the Illinois Department of Agriculture. Our warehouses are monitored by Fire Suppression Systems with central station monitoring to ensure that your inventory is always safe. Facilities are also equipped with indoor/outdoor docks as well as automatic levelers and trailer restraints. We have crane services available with a capacity of 5 tons. Our material handling fleet is comprised of late model, well-maintained equipment to assist in providing you with fast, reliable service.
